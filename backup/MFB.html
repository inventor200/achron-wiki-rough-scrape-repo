The Mobile Field Base (MFB) is a repair unit of the Humans. It is similar to the Blackbird. It is one of the few human units capable of carrying an AI's core into battle.

Abilities

Repair: 30HP/5

The MFB can repair all nearby units at a rate of 30HP every 2 seconds, for 5 per shot.

Recover Status: 25

The MFB can recover Status Effects from a target unit.
