The Macrofab is the Humans' heavy vehicle factory, and deploys advanced units such as the MFB, Blackbird, MAR, Frigate, and Heavy Cruiser. These units are generally more powerful and expensive than those from the Factory.

Abilities

Load Nuke

The Macrofab can load a nuke into a nearby Heavy Cruiser for 150 150.
