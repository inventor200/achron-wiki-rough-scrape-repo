low settings, single player playable, able to join multiplayer games, hosting bigger than small multiplayer maps problematic

  • AMD ATHLON 64 2300+, NVIDIA Geforce 7600GT, XP

Medium settings, single player playable, multiplayer can host and join maps, largest tested 512x512xdefault time

  • Intel Core 2 Duo T7700 (2.4GHz, laptop), nVidia GeForce 8600M GT, Vista
    • 1440x900 (native res) -- 30fps average, 20 fps minimum unless at medium settings
    • 1280x800 -- 30fps minimum, occasional unit animation stuttering

Low settings, single player hardly playable

  • Dell Inspiron E1705, Intel Core 2 Duo T7200 @ 2GHz, 4GB RAM, ATI Mobility Radeon X1400 256MB, Win7 64bit SP1
    • 1024x768 -- ~5fps
    • 800x600 -- ~10fps
    • 640x480 -- ~15fps, blurry text and graphics

High settings, single player playable, multiplayer can host and join maps, largest tested 256x256

  • Samsung R580, Intel Core i5 M430 @ 2.27Ghz, 4GB RAM, nVidia GeForce GT 330M 1GB, Gentoo
    • 1024x768 -- in window -- ~30-40fps
    • 1280x1024 -- fullscreen -- ~20-30fps

High settings, single player playable, multiplayer can host and join, largest tested 512x512xdefault time

  • Intel Core i7-2600k @ 3.4GHz, 8GB RAM, nVidia GeForce GTX 570
    • Windows 7
      • 1920x1080 -- fullscreen -- 60 fps, except for slight drops on Tomb of Heroes.
      • 1920x1080 -- fullscreen w/Fraps running -- 30-40 fps reliably, 60fps on less rendering-intensive maps
    • Ubuntu:
      • 1920x1080 -- windowed -- 60 fps (fullscreen stretches across multiple monitors, thus haven't tested)
