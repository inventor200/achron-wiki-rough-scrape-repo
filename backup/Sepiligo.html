The Sepiligo is an advanced Grekim Fighter-Bomber. It is effective against both air and ground targets, though slightly better against air targets. It is able to morph into a Gargantuan transport or split down into an Octo and a Pharo.

Thanks to it's large speed, range, and Chunkiness, it is extremely formidable when massed. However, it is now too expensive to commonly achieve critical mass. These properties also make it a popular target for Permacloning.
