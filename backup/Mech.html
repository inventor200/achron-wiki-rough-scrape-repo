The Mech is a robotic all-purpose marine, and builds advanced building for the Humans. It builds the Macrofab, Defence Turret, Slingshot, Teleporter, Chronoporter Gate, and the Carrier. It can also build Armories and Importers for 150% cost. It has limited fighting capabilities due to its low strength, health, and speed. It is the only human unit which does not take up unit reserves due to it being completely robotic.

The cost of mech composed only of liquid crystals, makes it a good sink for that resource.
