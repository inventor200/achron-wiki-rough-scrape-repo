The Pharopod is a mid-level Grekim cloaking bomber unit. It can also recover status effects.

Abilities

Cloaking: 5 (+1 every 3.1s)

This unit has the ability to cloak. While cloaked, this unit will still attack and may use any other abilities. A cloaked unit is invisible to all enemy units apart from units with the ability to detect invisible units such as the Tornade. Note: Because the Pharopod recharges energy while cloaked, cloak lasts much longer than the drain rate would suggest.

Recover Status: 4

The Pharopod can remove a status effect from a target unit.

Notes

Pharopods have many roles. They can serve as direct damage dealers, sneaky cloackers or defence against special abilities.

As of 0.9.7.0 pharopods are one of the main ways a grekim player can threaten other players. Shalkka 12:03, 25 August 2011 (UTC)
