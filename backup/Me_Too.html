Me Too

From Achron Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Setting up a hierachy leader to do various things and then later adding units to the hierachy before the hierachy has done anything. Can be used a chronoenergy cheap way of redoing a battle with more numerous units. Has high synergy with Optimal Mix.
