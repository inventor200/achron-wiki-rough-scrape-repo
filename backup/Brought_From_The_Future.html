Brought From The Future

From Achron Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Using means not available in the current time but bringing backup from the future. A lot of times the future contains a lot of resources being harvested and not used on anything. This allows for bringing a drastically powerfull force from a suprising source. The arriving group is causally connected to their home time. This means that while using future units may bring relief it means that there is less room to respond to changes when the future becomes nearer to the present.

Effective use of this tactic requires that production capacity in the future is easily available. Because of the large amount of unused resources this trick can be used to make larger scale Red Herrings. When the units are not new leads into Self help. There is also increased risk of ending up into a paradox, usually an Ontological Paradox.
