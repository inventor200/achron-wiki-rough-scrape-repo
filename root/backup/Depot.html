The Depot is a Vecgir factory that builds all their vehicles (every unit except the Vir class infantry and the Incepter troop transport). It allows any nearby Vir class to pilot a vehicle and must be adjacent to an Annex to construct vehicles and an Aerial Control Center for air vehicles. Right clicking on the Depot with a damaged vehicle will make the vehicle go into the Depot and be repaired for free.
