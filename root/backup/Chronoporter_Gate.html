A chronoporter gate (or just chronoporter) is an advanced CESO building that allows units to Chronoport. It is built by a Mech and can only be built after upgrading Gate Tech.

Abilities

Chronoportation

Allows units within its 23 unit chronoport radius to Chronoport.

Temporal Soliton Shield (TSS): 34 110

A chronoporter gate may grant TSS to a unit. Due to the recharge rate an individual Chronoporter gate can only put a TSS on a unit every 3 minutes, after the first two.
