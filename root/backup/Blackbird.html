The Blackbird is the Humans' aerospace repair vehicle, which is weaker but faster than the MFB. Because it is flying, it is able to repair anywhere on the map, without having to worry about cliffs in the way. It can also cloak itself.

Abilities

Repair: 12HP/5

The Blackbird can repair nearby units at a rate of 12HP every 2.2 seconds.

Cloaking: 5 (+0.5/s)

This unit has the ability to cloak. While cloaked, this unit will still attack and may use any other abilities. A cloaked unit is invisible to all enemy units apart from units with the ability to detect invisible units such as the Tornade.
