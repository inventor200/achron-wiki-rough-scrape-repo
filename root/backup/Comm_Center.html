The Comm Center is a building that can be built by a Marine. It is mobile and can enable Auto Hierarchy and Smart Idle. It is also able to fly while closed, and has a large vision range when activated, allowing it to act as a watch-tower. The Comm Center exists largely to reduce the micromanagement load on the player, though it can get in the way of a player who wants to be more hands-on.

Abilities

Auto Hierarchy

When turned on, this will make units automatically form hierarchies.

Smart Idle

When turned on, this will make units automatically ask for help for combat, healing, and other needs when not otherwise controlled.

Move

The Comm Center will close up and move to a selected location. While it is closed, it cannot use its other abilities. By holding shift and scrolling the mouse wheel, the destination height level can be set.
